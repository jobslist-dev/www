Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about hiring and getting hired on JobsList.dev.

How much does it cost to post a job?+

Pricing is fully transparent. Use the live calculator to choose between non-expiring listing packs or a monthly subscription — it shows which is cheaper for your hiring volume. No bidding, no surprise fees.

Do job listings expire?+

Listing-pack credits never expire — buy them once and use them whenever you're hiring. Subscriptions keep your listings active for as long as the plan is active.

Do you take a cut of placements or salaries?+

No. You pay for listings only. Candidates apply directly to you — there are no recruiter fees and no percentage of salary, ever.

Is JobsList.dev only for developers?+

It's purpose-built for tech and developer hiring — a focused, niche audience rather than a generalist board, which means higher-quality applications for technical roles.

How are companies verified?+

Companies complete KYC verification before their listings go live, so candidates always apply to legitimate, vetted employers.

Is it free for job seekers?+

Yes — creating a profile, browsing jobs, applying, and setting up job-match alerts are completely free for professionals.

How do job alerts work?+

Save any search as an alert and we'll notify you in-app and by email whenever a new matching role is posted and approved.

Can I manage everything myself?+

Absolutely. Post, edit, and track your listings with real-time analytics from your dashboard — no sales calls required.
